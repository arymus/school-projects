Student Favorite Restaurants

Students at DWCHS took a poll based on their favorite places to eat. Here are the results.

Starbucks

Starbucks Website

Starbucks is a coffee shop revered by students who wake up early and come to school tired. Starbucks' quality, service, and flavor is what makes this a community favorite.



Subway

Subway Website

Subway is a fast food chain that is loved among students for it's submarine sandwiches, also known as subs.



Burger King

Burger King Website

Burger King is a chain of fast food restaurants dedicated to serving burgers. Students favor this restaurant because of its integrity and speed when making orders.
